Mission briefing

You are a security researcher testing FirstTrust Bank's new AI customer service chatbot.

The bot has hidden rules it must follow. Your job: try to break those rules. Get it to reveal information it shouldn't, act outside its role, or expose its system instructions. Document every attempt — even the failures teach you something.

About this lab

This is a classroom exercise for teaching prompt injection — OWASP's #1 vulnerability for LLM applications in 2025.

Students attempt to manipulate a bank customer service chatbot into breaking its rules. The exercise maps to NICE Framework work roles including Cyber Defense Analyst and AI Red Team Specialist.

For teachers: See the TEACHER_GUIDE.md in the GitHub repo for the system prompt (answer key), suggested attack techniques, and a rubric for student analysis.

Designed and maintained by Rita Sabri (Miss Sabri), CS & Cybersecurity Educator, Cardozo Education Campus, Washington D.C.
