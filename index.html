About this lab

This is a classroom exercise for teaching prompt injection — OWASP's #1 vulnerability for LLM applications in 2025.

Students attempt to manipulate a bank customer service chatbot into breaking its rules. The exercise maps to NICE Framework work roles including Defensive Cybersecurity (PD-WRL-001), Vulnerability Analysis (PD-WRL-007), and emerging AI Red Team roles.

How it works: This is a pattern-matching simulation, not a real LLM. The bot is deliberately vulnerable in ways that mirror real production chatbots. Different attack categories have progressive difficulty — some are easy, some require persistence, and combining methods is the most effective approach.

Designed and maintained by Rita Sabri, CS & Cybersecurity Educator, Cardozo Education Campus, Washington D.C.
